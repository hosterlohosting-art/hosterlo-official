---------------------------------

WHAT'S WRONG: Only two hreflang tags exist:

  <link rel="alternate" hreflang="x-default" href="https://hosterlo.com/">

MISSING: hreflang tags for UK and Pakistan pages. Google won't
understand the relationship between your market pages.

  <link rel="alternate" hreflang="en-pk" href="https://hosterlo.com/pk/page/" />
  <link rel="alternate" hreflang="x-default" href="https://hosterlo.com/page/" />

  <!-- On UK pages (hosterlo.com/uk/*) -->
  <link rel="alternate" hreflang="en-us" href="https://hosterlo.com/page/" />
  <link rel="alternate" hreflang="en-gb" href="https://hosterlo.com/uk/page/" />
  <link rel="alternate" hreflang="en-pk" href="https://hosterlo.com/pk/page/" />
  <link rel="alternate" hreflang="x-default" href="https://hosterlo.com/page/" />

  <!-- On PK pages (hosterlo.com/pk/*) -->

BUG #5: SCHEMA areaServed STILL SAYS "Worldwide"
------------------------------------------------
LOCATION: Homepage JSON-LD schema
WHAT'S WRONG: The Organization schema says:
  "areaServed": "Worldwide"

If you're targeting US as primary market, this should be:
  "areaServed": {"@type": "Country", "name": "United States"}

Or keep "Worldwide" but add specific country pages as separate
LocalBusiness entries.

FIX: Update the Organization schema on each market page:
  - US page: "areaServed": {"@type": "Country", "name": "United States"}
  - UK page: "areaServed": {"@type": "Country", "name": "United Kingdom"}
  - PK page: "areaServed": {"@type": "Country", "name": "Pakistan"}

SEVERITY: Medium - Helps Google understand your target market
ESTIMATED FIX TIME: 30 minutes
