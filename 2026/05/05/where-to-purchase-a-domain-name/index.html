Where to Purchase a Domain Name: 7 Best Options Compared

May 5, 2026*8 min read*Hosterlo Team

Your domain name is the foundation of your online identity. But where should you buy it? We've compared the 7 best options for purchasing a domain name in 2026.

1. Hosterlo – Best Bundle (Free with Hosting)

If you need both hosting and a domain, Hosterlo is the smartest choice. All annual hosting plans include a free .COM domain, plus SSL, email, and cPanel. No separate registration needed. If you want to check domain availability, you can use our domain search and registration tool to see if your desired name is available before purchasing.

2. Namecheap – Best Budget Registrar

Namecheap offers competitive domain pricing with free WHOIS privacy. Great if you just need a domain without hosting.

3. Google Domains ? Squarespace Domains

Google Domains was acquired by Squarespace. Still a solid, no-upsell experience with transparent pricing and Google integration.

4. Cloudflare Registrar – At-Cost Pricing

Cloudflare sells domains at wholesale cost – no markup. Combined with their free CDN and DDoS protection, it's great for tech-savvy users.

5. GoDaddy – Largest Selection

GoDaddy offers the widest range of TLDs and a well-known brand. Watch out for aggressive upselling and higher renewal prices.

6. Porkbun – Best Hidden Gem

Porkbun offers very low prices, free WHOIS privacy, and a refreshingly simple interface. Great for bulk domain purchases.

7. Dynadot – Best for Domain Investors

Dynadot offers bulk management tools, auction marketplace, and competitive pricing for domain investors managing large portfolios.

Which Should You Choose?

If you're building a website, go with a host that bundles a free domain (like Hosterlo). If you just need a domain, Namecheap or Cloudflare offer the best standalone value.
