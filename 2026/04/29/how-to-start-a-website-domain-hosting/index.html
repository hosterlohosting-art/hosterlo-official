How to Start a Website With Domain and Hosting in 14 Easy Steps

April 29, 2026*10 min read*Hosterlo Team

Starting a website doesn't have to be complicated. In this guide, we'll walk you through 14 easy steps to launch your own website, from picking a domain to going live with your first page.

Step 1: Choose Your Website's Purpose

Before anything else, decide what your website will do. Is it a blog, an online store, a portfolio, or a business website? Your purpose determines your hosting needs, design, and content strategy.

Step 2: Pick a Domain Name

Your domain name is your website's address (like hosterlo.com). Choose something short, memorable, and relevant to your brand. A .com extension is always a safe choice.

Pro Tip: Hosterlo includes a free .COM domain with all annual hosting plans.

Step 3: Choose a Hosting Provider

Your web host is where your website lives. Look for fast load times (NVMe SSD), reliable uptime (99.9%+), free SSL, and 24/7 support. Hosterlo's hosting plans include all of these features.

Step 4: Register Your Domain

Once you've chosen a name, register it through your hosting provider or a domain registrar. If you're using Hosterlo's annual plans, your domain registration is free.

Step 5: Set Up Your Hosting Account

After purchasing, you'll receive login credentials for your control panel (cPanel). This is your dashboard for managing files, databases, emails, and more.

Step 6: Install WordPress

Most modern hosts offer one-click WordPress installation. With Hosterlo's WordPress hosting, WordPress comes pre-installed so you can start building immediately.

Step 7: Choose a Theme

Pick a professional theme that matches your brand. WordPress offers thousands of free and premium themes. Look for responsive, fast-loading designs.

Step 8: Install Essential Plugins

Plugins add functionality to your site. Start with: a security plugin, an SEO plugin (like Rank Math), a caching plugin for speed, and a backup plugin.

Step 9: Set Up SSL

SSL encrypts data between your site and visitors (the padlock icon). All Hosterlo plans include a free SSL certificate that can be activated with one click.

Step 10: Create Your Pages

Start with the essentials: Home, About, Contact, and your key service/product pages. Keep your content clear, focused, and valuable to your audience.

Step 11: Set Up Email

Create professional email addresses using your domain (e.g., hello@yourdomain.com). Hosterlo includes unlimited business email accounts with hosting plans.

Step 12: Configure SEO Basics

Set up your site title, meta descriptions, and submit your sitemap to Google Search Console. This helps search engines find and index your content.

Step 13: Test Everything

Check your site on different devices and browsers. Test your contact forms, navigation links, and page load speed. Fix any issues before launching.

Step 14: Launch and Promote!

Your site is ready! Share it on social media, submit it to directories, and start creating valuable content to attract visitors. Congratulations  you're officially online!
