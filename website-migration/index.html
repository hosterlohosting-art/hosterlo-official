How We Handle Your Transfer

We follow a structured migration checklist to prevent any file losses or email gaps. Your current site remains online the entire time.

1

Secure the Bundle

2

Provide Backups or Access Details

File and Database Transfer

Our support engineers transfer your files and import your MySQL databases onto our fast NVMe SSD servers. We configure directories and verify file permissions.

Answers to common concerns about switching hosting providers.

Will my website go offline during the migration? expand_more
No. Your current website remains fully online at your old provider while we copy and verify your files on Hosterlo. The switch only happens after everything is verified and the domain DNS is pointed.
How long does a migration take? expand_more
Most standard migrations are completed in less than 24 hours. Once DNS nameservers are pointed, it can take 12-24 hours for DNS updates to propagate globally.

Yes. If you are migrating a full cPanel account, we can migrate your email structures, mailboxes, and message history completely.
